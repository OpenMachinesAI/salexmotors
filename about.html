Our Mission

At Salex Motors, our mission is to advance bike transportation to new hights The name comes from our founder Alex and our supporter Sam

Our Mission

We are a community-driven initiative in Greater Victoria dedicated to transitioning to greener transportation. We develop innovative bike solutions and reinvest profits to benefit the community. Join us on this exciting journey toward a sustainable future!

Our Technology

We integrate the latest technologies in our products to ensure safety, efficiency, and connectivity. With features like advanced touch screens, voice command integration, and real-time monitoring, our vehicles are equipped to meet the needs of the modern rider.

The Future

Looking ahead, Salex Motors is focused on continuous innovation and sustainability. We are excited to explore new possibilities and expand our product line to offer even more groundbreaking solutions for our customers.
